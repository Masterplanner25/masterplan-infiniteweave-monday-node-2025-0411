Great call! Let's add the **Virality Calculation** to measure how a task, idea, or content spreads and gains traction.  
### **Virality Formula**  
We can base the virality calculation on key factors:  
- **Share Rate (`S`)** – How often something is shared.  
- **Engagement Rate (`E`)** – How many people interact with it.  
- **Conversion Rate (`C`)** – How many take meaningful action (e.g., signups, purchases).  
- **Time Factor (`T`)** – How quickly it spreads.  
#### **Formula for Virality Score (VS)**
\[
VS = \frac{(S \times E \times C)}{T + 1}
\]
- Higher `VS` = More viral potential.  
- Adding `1` to `T` prevents division errors.
